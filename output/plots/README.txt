Marcin_test_half_*:

Ratings only after 01.01.2015 00:00.
Then filtered movies with <= 50 ratings.
Used chi square test to test whether there is difference in distribution of first half of ratings and the second half (ordered by time).

Marcin_test_half_per_year_*:
Filtered movies released before 2015.


Marcin_test_year_frame_*:

Ratings only after 01.01.2015 00:00
Used chi square test to test whether there is significant difference in distribution of ratings given in the first year (bucket1) and later (bucket2).
Filtered movies with min(size(bucket1), size(bucket2)) < 20

Marcin test_year_frame_per_year_*:
Filtered movies released before 2015.
